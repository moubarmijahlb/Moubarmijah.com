Who are we?

We are a group computer scientists, looking to give the younger generation of girls opportunities to learn about the world of computers and programming.



How?

Throughout the "Moubarmijah" course, we teach high school students programming at a comfortable pace that won't interfere with their school studies.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is registering only for people who want to study computer science?

Anyone is welcome to register. We believe being a "Moubarmijah" is for everyone.

Is registering only for high school students?

We are receiving applicants from all age groups, as long as they have no coding knowledge. However, seat priority is for high school girls.

Will I be able to build a website/application if I take this course?

No. This is not a web development course. The aim of our course is to teach coding basics to build a solid coding base.

Can I know more details about the course material?

Check out the syllabus here

Why Python? Why not another language?

Python is widely used, and it is beginner-friendly.

Are the software needed free-of-charge?

All the software we're going to use is free to download.

In which language will the course be taught? Do I need to be fluent in English?

The slides will be in English, but the instructors will speak in Arabic. Basic English is enough.

Are you going to open a higher level course than beginner?

Yes. We are currently working on higher level courses in various programming topics.

What is the payment method?

Payment will be through Whish money transfer.
