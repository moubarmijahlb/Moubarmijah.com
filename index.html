Who are we?

We are a group computer scientists, looking to give the younger generation of girls opportunities to learn about the world of computers and programming.



How?

Throughout the "Moubarmijah" course, we teach high school students programming at a comfortable pace that won't interfere with their school studies.

about our course

What we Offer

Our Programming Course

Python Beginner Course - L1

A beginner course in which you will learn programming from scratch using the programming language "Python". At the end of the course you should be able to complete the final project along with your teammates.

Who can join the course

  • Highschool girls in grades 10, 11 and 12
  • Students of any background
  • Students who have motivation to learn

What you will learn

  • Python Syntax
  • Programming Logic
  • Programming Careers
  • Teamwork

Course details

  • Course Duration: 6 weeks
  • Two hours per week
  • Weekly assignments
  • Final project
